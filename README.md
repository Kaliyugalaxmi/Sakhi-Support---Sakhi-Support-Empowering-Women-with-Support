**Sakhi Support** is a web platform designed to help women easily discover **government welfare schemes, job opportunities, safety helplines, and community support** in one place.

Built during the **48-hour She Ships Global Hackathon**, the platform aims to bridge the information gap and empower women with access to opportunities, resources, and support networks.
