# 🌸 Sakhi Support  

**Sakhi Support** is a web platform designed to help women easily discover **government welfare schemes, job opportunities, safety helplines, and community support** in one place.

Built during the **48-hour She Ships Global Hackathon**, the platform aims to bridge the information gap and empower women with access to opportunities, resources, and support networks.

---

# 💜 About the Hackathon

This project was built during the **She Ships Global Hackathon**, a global online event celebrating **International Women’s Day**.

Participants from around the world created impactful projects addressing:

- Gender equity
- Women’s safety
- Access to opportunities
- Social empowerment

The goal was to **design and ship a real project within 48 hours** that solves a meaningful problem affecting women.

---

# 🚨 Problem

Many women face difficulties accessing opportunities and support due to:

- Lack of awareness about government schemes
- Limited access to job opportunities
- Difficulty understanding eligibility requirements
- Lack of supportive communities for guidance
- Important helplines not easily accessible

Because of this, many women miss opportunities that could improve their **financial independence, education, and safety**.

---

# 💡 Solution

**Sakhi Support** provides a **centralized platform** where women can:

- Discover government schemes
- Find job opportunities
- Check eligibility easily
- Access emergency helplines
- Connect with a supportive community

The goal is to make important resources **simple, accessible, and empowering**.

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 🔎 Government Scheme Finder

Users can explore various government schemes categorized by:

- Health
- Education
- Employment
- Financial Assistance
- Social Welfare

Each scheme includes:

- Benefits
- Eligibility criteria
- Required documents
- Application steps

---

## 💼 Job Opportunities for Women

The platform also provides a **job discovery section** where users can explore employment opportunities designed to support women’s financial independence.

Examples include:

- Anganwadi Worker
- Community Health Worker
- Skill Development Jobs
- Government Support Programs

Each job listing provides:

- Job description
- Eligibility requirements
- Application information


---

## ☎️ Emergency Helplines

Quick access to important women safety helplines such as:

- Women Helpline
- Police Emergency
- Domestic Violence Support
- Legal Assistance

Providing immediate support in urgent situations.

---

## 👩‍👩‍👧 Community Support

Sakhi Support includes a **community section** where women can:

- Share experiences
- Ask questions
- Support each other
- Discuss opportunities and challenges

This helps create a **safe and empowering digital environment**.

---

## ⚙️ Admin Dashboard

An **Admin Panel** allows administrators to manage the platform by:

- Adding new schemes
- Updating eligibility criteria
- Adding job opportunities
- Managing scheme and job data

This ensures the platform remains **updated and relevant**.
